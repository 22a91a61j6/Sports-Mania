TEAM SPORTS

Team sports are competitive activities where players collaborate to achieve a common goal.They require communication, cooperation, and strategic planning, making them a great way to develop social and leadership skills. Here are some examples:

Teamwork
Leadership
Commitment
Time Management

INDIVIDUAL SPORTS

Individual sports are activities where participants compete on their own, focusing on personal performance and improvement. These sports allow athletes to set personal goals, develop self-discipline, and hone specific skills. Here are some examples:

Self-Esteem
Personal Responsibility
Time Management
Mental Toughness
